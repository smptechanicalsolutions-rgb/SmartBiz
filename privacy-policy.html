Privacy Policy

How SmartBiz handles information when you use the invoice, inventory, and catalog features.

Effective date: 24 May 2026

1. Who we are

SmartBiz (“we”, “the app”) is business software for creating GST invoices, quotations, delivery challans, inventory records, and price-list catalogs. This policy describes data practices for the web application operated by the SmartBiz project (developer: Shubham Patil).

2. Information you provide

You may enter or store:

  • Business profile: company name, GSTIN, PAN, address, phone, email, logo.
  • Customer and supplier details on invoices and challans.
  • Product and inventory data (names, rates, HSN, stock).
  • Catalog / menu items and prices.
  • Account credentials (email, password hash on the server when you register).

You are responsible for accuracy and lawful use of customer data (including consent where required under applicable law, e.g. India's DPDP Act 2023 for personal data).

3. Where data is stored

  • On your device: Browser localStorage holds drafts, history, settings, and catalog data for offline-style use.
  • On our server (when enabled): Login sessions, document history API, and account records may be stored on servers you deploy or we host for sync.
  • Exports: PDF and JSON exports are generated on your device; we do not receive them unless you send them to us.

4. How we use information

We use data only to:

  • Provide app features (invoicing, previews, history, currency conversion).
  • Authenticate your account and sync settings where configured.
  • Improve stability and fix errors (e.g. server logs without invoice line details where possible).

We do not sell your business or customer lists to third parties for marketing.

5. Third-party services

  • Exchange rates: Currency conversion may call public rate APIs (e.g. open.er-api.com) with INR amounts — no customer names are sent.
  • Fonts & icons: CDN providers (Google Fonts, Font Awesome) may receive your IP address as with any website.
  • Email (optional): If email features are enabled on your deployment, your operator's email provider processes messages you send.

6. Cookies and local storage

We use essential cookies for login sessions where authentication is enabled. Settings and document data are stored in local storage until you clear them or use in-app reset/backup tools.

7. Security

Passwords are stored hashed (bcrypt) on the server. Use a strong password and HTTPS in production. You should backup important data regularly via Settings → Download backup.

8. Retention & deletion

  • Local data remains until you clear browser storage or use Clear temporary data in Settings.
  • Server-side account data may be deleted on account closure request — contact us below.
  • Deleted test documents (TEST-*) are removed from history when you delete them in the app.

9. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we hold about your account. Business data you entered can be exported from the app at any time.

10. Children

SmartBiz is intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

11. Changes

We may update this policy; the “Effective date” above will change. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.
