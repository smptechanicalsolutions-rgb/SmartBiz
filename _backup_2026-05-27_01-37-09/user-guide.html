Getting started

Your first-time checklist before creating any document.

  1. Open Home from the left sidebar — this is your main dashboard.
  2. Tap the person icon (top) or go to Settings → Personal Information to add your company name, GSTIN, address, and logo.
  3. Open Menu to see the demo food catalog, or Inventory to add products you sell (name, rate, HSN, tax).
  4. Pick a document type from Quick Actions (Invoice, Delivery Challan, Quotation, etc.).
  5. Fill the form, choose a layout/format, then tap Preview Document.
  6. On the preview page use Download / Share or Print to save or send the PDF.
  7. Not ready to enter real data? Open the test documents section — four TEST-* samples share one product (2 × ₹350), ₹10 delivery, and GST (invoice total ₹837.80 ≈ $10).

Creating a tax invoice

GST invoices with items, tax, signature, and stamp.

  1. From Home, tap New Invoice or open Invoice Layouts and pick Format 1, 2, or 3.
  2. On the editor (GST Invoice Generator), use tabs: Party Details for company & client, Transaction Details for invoice lines and totals.
  3. Tap Add Company / Add Client to enter details, or load saved profiles from the dropdown.
  4. Under Invoice Info, set invoice number, date, due date, and place of supply.
  5. Add products (name, qty, rate, HSN, tax %). Use + for more rows.
  6. Optional: Taxes, Payment instructions, Shipping charges, Watermark, Stamp.
  7. Draw or load a signature in the signature pad section.
  8. Select format at the bottom and click Preview Document.

Tip: Use Document History on the editor to reopen past invoices without retyping.

Delivery challan

Record goods dispatched without a tax invoice.

  1. From Home → Delivery Challan or sidebar workflows.
  2. View past challans in the history list; search by client or number.
  3. Create new: use the editor with delivery challan formats (Format 3, 4, or 11).
  4. Fill sender, receiver, items shipped, and vehicle/transport details as shown on the form.
  5. Preview and print — same Back button returns to the previous screen.
Delivery challans

Quotation

Send prices before confirming a sale.

  1. Home → Quotation to open quotation history.
  2. Create a new quotation from the editor; choose Format 5, 6, or 12.
  3. Add client, items, validity date, and terms — structure is similar to invoices.
  4. Preview and share PDF with your client.
  5. Later, use Convert Document on Home to turn an accepted quotation into an invoice.
Quotations

Propharma invoice

Dedicated flow for pharmacy-style billing.

  1. Home → Propharma Invoice.
  2. Follow the on-screen fields for batch, expiry, MRP, and line items as applicable.
  3. Preview using propharma formats (7, 8, or 9) for print-ready layout.

Address labels

Save and print frequent shipping addresses.

  1. Home → Address.
  2. Enter label name, party name, company, full address, city, pincode.
  3. Save to your address book; Search saved entries later.
  4. Print or export labels for couriers and packages.
Address labels

Convert document

Reuse data from one document type in another.

  1. Home → Convert Document.
  2. Pick a saved document from the list (must exist in history).
  3. Choose the target type — e.g. Quotation → Invoice.
  4. Confirm; the editor opens with fields pre-filled. Review totals and preview.

Tip: Create at least one invoice or quotation first so history has something to convert.

Document preview, PDF & print

What you see after tapping Preview Document.

  1. The preview opens in a new tab with your chosen format layout.
  2. Use ← Back (top-left) to return without losing data in the editor.
  3. Download / Share — save PDF or share via apps on your device.
  4. Print (Vector PDF) — best quality for professional printing.
  5. Edit Invoice — jumps back to the editor with your data.
  6. Watermark and stamp appear on the document if you enabled them in the editor.

Inventory & stock history

Products, quantities, and stock movements.

  1. Sidebar → Inventory to add and edit products.
  2. Set name, SKU, category, stock quantity, purchase and sale rates.
  3. When creating invoices, product templates can speed up line-item entry.
  4. Stock History in the sidebar tracks ins/outs and adjustments over time.

Editing one item (inside a category)

  1. Find the gray item card under a category heading.
  2. Item name — what customers see (e.g. Butter Chicken).
  3. Description — optional line under the name on preview.
  4. SKU / code — optional internal code (ST-01, MN-02).
  5. Price (₹) — amount in rupees; Settings can display as $ or € on preview.
  6. Tap + Add item on the category row for another dish or product.
  7. Red trash on an item or category deletes it (category deletes all items inside).

First visit

Settings

Currency, defaults, and app behaviour.

  1. Sidebar → Settings.
  2. Appearance — display currency and defaults (₹ stored; $/€ converted live).
  3. Documents & defaults — auto-save, default GST %, layout, watermark.
  4. Company profile — opens Personal Information (GSTIN, logo, address).

Personal & company information

Your profile shown on invoices and cards.

  1. Tap the profile / edit icon in the header or open Personal Information from the menu.
  2. Fill legal business name, GSTIN, PAN, address, phone, email, and upload logo.
  3. Save — these details auto-fill on new invoices where supported.
Personal info

Signature & round stamp

Authorize documents like a physical stamp.

  1. In the invoice editor, scroll to Signature — draw on the pad or load a saved signature.
  2. Tap Save Signature; use Manage to pick from the gallery.
  3. For the round seal: tap Add Stamp, enter top ring text (company), bottom text (e.g. OFFICIAL), center city.
  4. Choose stamp colour and save; it overlays the signature area on preview.
  5. Adjust stamp size in settings if the app stores a size preference.

Tip: On preview pages only one Back button appears (top-left) — use it to return to the editor.

Document history

Find and reopen past work.

  1. On the invoice editor, tap Document History.
  2. Browse by client name, invoice number, or date.
  3. Open a row to reload that document into the editor or preview.
  4. History is stored on the server and in your browser; refresh Home to see the latest list.

Login & account

Optional sign-in for sync and security.

  1. Tap Login / Sign up on Home or the header when shown.
  2. Create an account or sign in with your email.
  3. After login, your session keeps preferences; use Logout on shared devices.
  4. Forgot password flows are on the login page links.
