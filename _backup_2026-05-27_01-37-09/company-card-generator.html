How to Create Your Business Card

Follow these simple steps to design a professional business card:

  1. Fill in your company name and your name
  2. Add your job title, phone number, and email
  3. Optionally add your website, company logo, and city
  4. Choose a design color that matches your brand
  5. Preview your card on the right side in real-time
  6. Click Download to save as image or Share to send it
